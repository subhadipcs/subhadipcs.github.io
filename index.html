About

Hello! I am a Ph.D. scholar in Computer Science at Indian Institute of Technology Palakkad, advised by Dr. Krishnamoorthy Dinesh. My research area is Boolean Circuit Complexity.

My primary research interests lie in theoretical computer science, particularly in Boolean circuit complexity and communication complexity. I am interested in understanding the limitations of Boolean circuits, including questions related to circuit lower bounds, separations between complexity classes, and how circuit depth and size affect computational efficiency.
